Paneer-Biriyani

Paneer Biriyani

Decription

A Dish made of rice and Paneer(also called Cottage-Cheese) popular all over Indian Subcontinent and abroad

    Ingredients

    For Marination

  • 1 cup curd / yogurt, thick
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4th tsp Turmeric
  • 3/4th tsp Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp mint / pudina, chopped
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 15 cubes paneer/ cottage cheese
  • ½ onion, petals
  • ½ capsicum, cubed

    • For Rice

  • 6 cup water
  • 2 pods cardamom
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 2 bay leaf / tej patta
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1 chilli, slit
  • 1 cup basmati rice, soaked 20 minutes

    • For Biriyani

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp ghee / clarified butter
  • 2 bay leaf / tej patta
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 pod black cardamom
  • 1 mace / javitri
  • 2 pods cardamom
  • 1 tsp shah jeera
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander, chopped
  • 2 tbsp mint / pudina, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fried onion
  • pinch biryani masala
  • 2 tbsp saffron milk
  • 1 tsp ghee / clarified butter
  • ¼ cup water

      • Steps

    1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 1 cup curd, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, ¼ tsp turmeric, ¾ tsp chilli powder and 2 tbsp biryani masala.
    2. Also add 2 tbsp coriander, 2 tbsp mint, 1 tsp oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice and 1 tsp salt.
    3. Whisk and combine well making sure the spices are well combined.
    4. Further add 15 cubes paneer, ½ onion and ½ capsicum.
    5. Mix gently making sure everything is combined well.
    6. Cover and marinate for 30 minutes.

      7. Biriyani Rice Prep

    7. Firstly, in a large vessel take 6 cup water.
    8. Add spices like 2 pods cardamom, 4 cloves, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 bay leaf, ½ tsp pepper.
    9. Also add 1 tsp salt, 2 tsp oil and 1 chilli.
    10. Boil for 2 minutes or until flavours is in the water.
    11. Add in 1 cup basmati rice and stir well. make sure to soak rice for at least 20 minutes.
    12. Boil for 3 minutes or until rice is half cooked. do not cook fully.
    13. Drain off the rice and keep aside.

      14. Paneer Biriyani Preperation

    14. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and 1 tsp ghee.
    15. Saute 2 bay leaf, 1 star anise, 1 pod black cardamom, 1 mace, 2 pods cardamom and 1 tsp shah jeera.
    16. Add ½ onion and saute until it turns golden brown.
    17. Also add 1 tomato and saute until it turns soft and mushy.
    18. Add in marinated paneer and saute well.
    19. Saute until the oil separates from sides. do not overcook as paneer turns rubbery.
    20. Further spread prepared rice uniformly.
    21. Also top with 2 tbsp coriander, 2 tbsp mint and 2 tbsp fried onion.
    22. Further sprinkle pinch biryani masala, 2 tbsp saffron milk, 1 tsp ghee and ¼ cup water.
    23. Cover with aluminium foil and close the lid. you can also use the dough to seal.
    24. Simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is cooked fully.
    25. Finally, enjoy paneer biryani with raita and slices of onion.
